PEYA-KING Cash

The financial product Peya-King Cash which was introduced to the market in the latter part of the last year enables customers to borrow cash against vehicles in their possession without requiring any further security. The most attractive feature of this product is the fact that the cash is released to the customer within one hour.

  • No guarantors needed.
  • Cash released within one hour.
  • Repayment facility at any branch.
  • Fast  approval and efficient service. 
  • Must be a sri lankan citizen between the age limit 18 - 60 years.
  • Clear purpose of applying the loan.
  • Manufacturing year of the registered bikes and three wheelers should be within 05 years up to the date of disbursement.
